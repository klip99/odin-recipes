Tantalizingly Tangy Meatloaf

This meatloaf with a tangy sauce with will keep your guests coming back for more. The sauce contains pineapple preserves, brown sugar, and ketchup. It glazes up really nice over the meatloaf. I've never had leftovers — even when I've tripled the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • garlic powder to taste
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup pineapple preserves
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, garlic powder, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well, and place into a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven, 30 to 50 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, in a separate medium bowl, stir together ketchup, brown sugar, and pineapple preserves. Pour over meatloaf about 20 minutes before removing it from the oven.
